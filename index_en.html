Services for Companies

Our tailored language and cultural training programs are designed to serve your organization's unique needs. Services are implemented on a flexible schedule online.

Language Training - Finnish, English, and Chinese

Team language skills are a key factor in intercultural communication and promoting successful international cooperation. Ginkgo offers language courses tailored to personnel skills and the client's unique situation and needs.

Cultural Coaching

Operating in new cultural environments can be challenging. We offer training related to local customs and corporate culture. Packages can also include language lessons and consulting services.

Marketing and Communications (China)

We help Finnish companies succeed in Chinese digital channels. We offer a clear overview of the Chinese social media ecosystem, a tailored marketing strategy, and support for reaching target audiences and targeting advertising.

Consulting and Translation Services

We offer advice and support for traveling, settling, and navigating daily life in Finland or China. You also get market research services and up-to-date industry reviews from us. Additionally, we offer translation services (English, Finnish, Chinese). We can translate the company name, website, and other important materials.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who are the services for?

We serve Finnish companies that have Chinese clients, partners, or plans to expand to the Chinese market. Our clients include tourism companies, educational organizations, and industrial players. We also provide services to Chinese companies interested in the Finnish market. We also offer training packages and language classes for private individuals.

What is the benefit of training and coaching for business?

Intercultural competence strengthens trust, prevents misunderstandings, and enhances cooperation. Correct communication and targeted marketing help to stand out and build long-term customer relationships.

Can the training be tailored to fit our industry and personnel?

Yes. Our services and products are tailored according to the client's industry and personnel. Content is always built considering the client's goals and situation.

How does cooperation proceed if we are interested?

We begin with a short, free initial consultation. Based on that, we will propose a service package tailored to your needs within a fast timeframe — typically within three business days. The delivery can be carried out fully remotely, according to your schedule.
